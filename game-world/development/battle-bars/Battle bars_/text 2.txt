Visual Style: 2D Pixel Art (Retro/Cyberpunk overlay)
Core Loop: Train AI Avatar \rightarrow Battle Rivals \rightarrow Claim Territory \rightarrow Evolve Logic.
1. THE AVATAR: "The Proxy"
You do not rap directly. You act as the Manager/Trainer for a digital entity called a Proxy.
 * Genesis: Player selects a base archetype (e.g., The Conscious Poet, The Trap Lord, The Abstract Nerd, The Mumble God).
 * The Brain (LLM): The Proxy starts with a "Empty Context Window" (a blank brain). It creates generic, weak bars.
 * Evolution: As you play, you fill its context slots with specific metadata, slang, and rhyme schemes, effectively "fine-tuning" the personality.
2. TRAINING MECHANICS (The "Gym")
Instead of grinding XP, you grind Data. You teach the AI how to sound like you.
 * "Mad-Lib" Injection: Minigames where the system gives a setup, and the user provides the punchline words. The AI learns your vocabulary preferences from these inputs.
 * Phonics Puzzles: Rhythm games where you match rhyming sounds. Success unlocks "Complex Rhyme Schemes" (e.g., multisyllabic rhymes) for your Proxy to use in battle.
 * Topic Drills: An NPC challenges you: "Tell me about the struggle." You input keywords. The AI synthesizes this into a verse. If itâ€™s weak, you "reroll" and correct it. The AI remembers the correction for the next battle.
3. THE BATTLE SYSTEM (PvE & PvP)
Battles are automated spectacles based on your training preparation.
 * The Setup:
   * Challenger: "I drop a beat (BPM 140)."
   * Defender: Accepts.
   * Topic Engine: A random theme is generated (e.g., "Crypto Crash," "Waffle House at 3 AM," "Existential Dread").
 * The Exchange:
   * The two Proxies generate lyrics in real-time (using the LLM backend).
   * Visuals: Text bubbles appear over heads with kinetic typography (shaking text for yelling, glitch text for complex burns).
   * Crowd Reaction: In PvP, other players can spectate and spam emotes. The "Heat Meter" tracks rhyme density and crowd hype.
 * Win Condition: Logic-based scoring (Rhyme Density + Relevancy to Topic) OR Crowd Voting.
4. PROGRESSION & WORLD MAP
 * The World: An open pixel-art city divided into Districts (e.g., Boom Bap Burrows, Trap Towers, Lo-Fi Lounge).
 * Territory Control: Defeat the "District Kingpin" (Boss NPC) to claim that block. Your Proxyâ€™s tag (graffiti) appears on the walls of that area for all other players to see.
 * Loot (The "Cartridges"):
   * Flow Chips: Unlocks faster delivery speeds (triplet flows, chopper style).
   * Vocab Packs: Rare data drops containing specific slang dictionaries (e.g., "1990s NY Slang Pack" or "Cyber-Techno Jargon").
   * Voice Skins: Change the TTS (Text-to-Speech) output of your Proxy.
5. SYNERGY WITH "BEATMON"
 * Cross-Compatibility: Beats collected in Beatmon can be imported here to serve as the backing tracks for battles.
 * Ecosystem: Beatmon is for Producers (Music). Bar-Botech is for Lyricists/Promoters (Vocals). They feed each other.
