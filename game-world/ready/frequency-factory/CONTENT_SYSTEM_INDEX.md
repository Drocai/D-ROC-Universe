## üí° CONTENT CREATION TIPS

### Short-Form Video Best Practices
1. **Hook in 3 seconds** - Start with question, bold statement, or visual surprise
2. **Text overlays essential** - 85% watch without sound
3. **Trending audio** - Use platform's trending sounds library
4. **Pattern interrupts** - Change scene/angle every 3-5 seconds
5. **Clear CTA** - End with specific action (link in bio, follow, comment)

### Caption Writing Formula
```
[HOOK LINE - Curiosity or value proposition]

[CONTEXT - 1-2 sentences of setup]

[MAIN CONTENT - Key points, tips, or story]

[ENGAGEMENT QUESTION - Prompt for comments]

[CALL TO ACTION - What to do next]

[HASHTAGS - 5-10 relevant tags]
```

### Community Management
- **Respond within 1 hour** during business hours
- **Like every comment** on your posts
- **Ask follow-up questions** to commenters
- **Feature user content** with permission
- **Create inside jokes/references** to build culture
