## ‚≠ê HOW TO RATE TRACKS

### **The Rating Process**

**Step 1: Listen**
- Track plays automatically
- Minimum 30 seconds required
- Listen to full track for best results
- Pause/play as needed

**Step 2: Rate 10 Categories (0-10 each)**

1. **Production Quality** - Mix, mastering, sound design
2. **Vocals** - Performance, delivery, technique
3. **Melody** - Hook quality, catchiness
4. **Lyrics** - Writing, flow, meaning
5. **Originality** - Uniqueness, innovation
6. **Vibe** - Feeling, atmosphere
7. **Replay Value** - Would you listen again?
8. **Commercial Appeal** - Radio/playlist potential
9. **Artistic Merit** - Creative vision
10. **Overall** - Your overall impression

**Step 3: Add Feedback (Optional)**
- Leave text comment
- Be constructive and specific
- Help artists improve

**Step 4: Submit**
- Click "Submit Rating"
- Tokens awarded instantly
- Next track loads automatically

---

### **Rating Tips**

**Be Honest:**
- Your genuine opinion matters
- Don't inflate scores
- Consider the genre's standards

**Be Fair:**
- Judge music on its own merits
- Don't penalize unfamiliar genres
- Separate personal taste from quality

**Be Constructive:**
- Explain your ratings
- Highlight strengths AND weaknesses
- Offer specific feedback

**Be Consistent:**
- Use the full 0-10 scale
- Reserve 10/10 for exceptional work
- Save 0/10 for truly flawed tracks

## üéµ SUBMITTING YOUR MUSIC

### **Before You Submit**

**Requirements:**
- 100 tokens (submission fee)
- Audio file or streaming link
- Track metadata (title, artist, genre, etc.)
- Cover art
- All necessary rights/licenses

**Check:**
- [x] I own this music OR have proper licenses
- [x] Audio quality is good (no excessive distortion)
- [x] Track length under 5 minutes
- [x] Metadata is accurate
- [x] Content follows guidelines

---

### **Submission Process**

**Step 1: Prepare Your Track**
- Export audio (MP3, WAV, or upload to Spotify/Apple Music)
- Create cover art (square image, min 1000x1000px)
- Gather metadata (genre, BPM, key, etc.)
- Write description

**Step 2: Submit Through Platform**
1. Click "Submit Track"
2. Pay 100 tokens
3. Upload audio OR paste streaming link
4. Fill out form:
   - Title
   - Artist name
   - Genre (select from list)
   - Optional: BPM, key, description
5. Upload cover art
6. Add streaming links (Spotify, Apple Music, etc.)
7. Review and submit

**Step 3: Wait for Approval**
- Review within 48 hours
- Check for guideline compliance
- Verify copyright/licensing
- Approved tracks go live next day at 3pm EST

**Step 4: Track Activation**
- Email notification when live
- Track enters rating queue
- Users start rating
- You can watch stats in real-time

---

## üìà UNDERSTANDING RATINGS

### **How Ratings Work**

**Rating Scale:**
- Each category: 0-10
- Total categories: 10
- Maximum score: 100
- Minimum score: 0

**Average Rating:**
- Sum of all user ratings
- Divided by number of raters
- Updated in real-time
- Displayed as X/100

**Rating Distribution:**
- See breakdown of scores
- Understand audience reception
- Identify strengths/weaknesses

---

### **What Ratings Mean**

**90-100: Exceptional**
- Certification territory
- Industry-ready quality
- Commercial potential
- Highly recommended

**80-89: Great**
- Strong track
- Good production
- Room for minor improvements
- Playlist-worthy

**70-79: Good**
- Solid effort
- Some strengths
- Noticeable weaknesses
- Has potential

**60-69: Decent**
- Average quality
- Mixed reception
- Needs work
- Learning opportunity

**50-59: Below Average**
- Significant issues
- Underdeveloped
- Requires improvement

**0-49: Poor**
- Major problems
- Unfinished quality
- Needs serious work

---

### **Interpreting Feedback**

**Look for Patterns:**
- Multiple users mention same issue?
- Consistent praise for certain elements?
- Balance of opinions?

**Categories to Watch:**
- Low production = mixing/mastering issues
- Low vocals = performance/technique needs work
- Low melody = hook isn't catchy enough
- Low replay = doesn't stick with listeners

**Constructive Criticism:**
- Don't take it personally
- Focus on actionable feedback
- Use it to improve
- Thank honest reviewers
