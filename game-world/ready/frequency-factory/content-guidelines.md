### **PROHIBITED CONTENT**

‚ùå **Copyright Infringement:**
- Stolen beats or instrumentals
- Unauthorized samples
- Other artists' music claimed as yours
- Copyrighted material without license

‚ùå **Hate Speech:**
- Racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia
- Religious intolerance
- Ethnic slurs or attacks
- Nazi imagery or hate symbols

‚ùå **Illegal Content:**
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Human trafficking content
- Drug manufacturing instructions
- Terrorist propaganda

‚ùå **Harassment:**
- Diss tracks targeting platform users
- Doxxing (sharing personal info)
- Threats or intimidation
- Revenge content

‚ùå **Low-Quality Spam:**
- Random noise submissions
- Test tracks ("testing 1-2-3")
- Duplicate submissions
- AI-generated spam music

‚ùå **Misleading Content:**
- Fake artist names (impersonation)
- Misleading genres
- Clickbait titles
- False metadata

---

### **SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS**

**Technical Standards:**
- Minimum audio quality: 128kbps MP3 or equivalent
- Maximum length: 5 minutes
- Clear audio (no excessive distortion unless artistic)
- Proper mixing (listenable volume)

**Metadata Requirements:**
- Accurate track title
- Correct artist name
- Proper genre classification
- Real release year (if applicable)

**Content Labeling:**
- Mark explicit content clearly
- Identify features/collaborators
- Credit producers/beat makers
- Link to official releases

## ‚≠ê RATING GUIDELINES

### **HOW TO RATE EFFECTIVELY**

**Be Honest:**
- Rate based on your genuine opinion
- Don't inflate/deflate scores unfairly
- Consider the track's intent and genre

**Be Constructive:**
- Explain your rating (optional but encouraged)
- Highlight what works AND what doesn't
- Offer specific feedback when possible

**Be Fair:**
- Judge music on its own merits
- Don't penalize for genre preference
- Rate production, performance, creativity separately

**Be Respectful:**
- Critique the music, not the artist
- Avoid personal attacks
- Remember there's a human behind every track

---

### **RATING CATEGORIES EXPLAINED**

**1. Production Quality (0-10)**
- Mix quality
- Mastering
- Sound design
- Audio clarity
- Professional polish

**2. Vocals (0-10)**
- Performance quality
- Vocal technique
- Delivery and emotion
- Pitch accuracy
- Style and tone

**3. Melody (0-10)**
- Hook catchiness
- Memorability
- Musical composition
- Chord progressions
- Instrumental melody

**4. Lyrics (0-10)**
- Writing quality
- Storytelling
- Wordplay and flow
- Message and meaning
- Originality

**5. Originality (0-10)**
- Uniqueness
- Innovation
- Fresh perspective
- Genre-bending
- Artistic vision

**6. Vibe (0-10)**
- Emotional impact
- Atmosphere and mood
- Energy level
- Cohesiveness
- Feeling and groove

**7. Replay Value (0-10)**
- Would you listen again?
- Long-term appeal
- Doesn't get old
- Playlist-worthy
- Addictive quality

**8. Commercial Appeal (0-10)**
- Radio potential
- Playlist viability
- Mainstream accessibility
- Market readiness
- Industry appeal

**9. Artistic Merit (0-10)**
- Creative depth
- Artistic vision
- Cultural relevance
- Thought-provoking
- Pushes boundaries

**10. Overall (0-10)**
- Holistic impression
- Complete package
- Total experience
- Gut feeling
- Would you recommend?

---

### **PROHIBITED RATING BEHAVIOR**

‚ùå **Rating Manipulation:**
- Creating fake accounts to inflate scores
- Coordinated rating attacks
- Vote brigading
- Rating in exchange for money/favors
- Bot-generated ratings

‚ùå **Dishonest Rating:**
- Rating tracks you didn't listen to
- Random/spam ratings
- Intentionally tanking scores (except genuine opinion)
- Giving 0s or 10s without justification

‚ùå **Harassment:**
- Using ratings to attack artists
- Leaving abusive comments
- Targeting specific users repeatedly
- Discriminatory rating patterns
