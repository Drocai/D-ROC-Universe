## üé® CONTENT CREATION QUICK TIPS

### Instagram Best Practices
- **First 3 seconds:** Must stop the scroll
- **Captions:** Start strong, break into short paragraphs
- **Hashtags:** 8-10, mix of big + niche
- **CTA:** Always include clear next action
- **Carousel:** End with CTA slide
- **Reels:** Text overlays essential (85% watch muted)

### TikTok Best Practices
- **Hook in 3 seconds or die**
- **Use trending audio** (TikTok Creative Center)
- **Text overlays readable without sound**
- **Pattern interrupts every 3-5 seconds**
- **Point up gesture for CTA**
- **Face on camera** (algorithm favors it)
- **Post 2-4x daily for algorithm favor**

### YouTube Best Practices
- **First 30 seconds hook** or viewers leave
- **Timestamps in description** (searchability + retention)
- **Thumbnail = 80% of click-through**
- **Title: Hook + Promise + Keyword**
- **End screens:** Subscribe + Next video
- **Community tab:** Engage between uploads

### Email Best Practices
- **Subject under 50 characters**
- **Mobile-first design** (70% open on mobile)
- **One CTA per email**
- **Conversational tone**
- **Short paragraphs** (2-3 sentences max)
- **P.S. always** (people read it)
