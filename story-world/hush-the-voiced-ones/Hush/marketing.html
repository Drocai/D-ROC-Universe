Brand & Marketing

Strategy

Target Audience:

  • Fantasy readers ages 16-35
  • Fans of dystopian and speculative fiction
  • Readers drawn to strong female protagonists

Key Messaging:

  • "In a world of silence, a whisper is a revolution."
  • "Find your voice. Change your world."
  • "Some sounds can't be silenced."

Marketing Channels:

  • Social media with sound-reactive content
  • Interactive website and "Find Your Sound" quiz
  • Bookstore partnerships with audio installations
  • Influencer collaborations with voice actors

Brand Color Palette

Obsidian Black

Opal Iridescence

Electric Blue

Amber Gold

Visual Elements

Sound Waves

Cracked Marble

Iridescent Accents

Brand Voice

Poetic yet accessible, mysterious but not obtuse, emotionally resonant without being melodramatic, balancing darkness with moments of beauty and hope.
