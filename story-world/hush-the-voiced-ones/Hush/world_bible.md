# HUSH: The Voiced Ones - World Bible

This document details the world, characters, and lore of "HUSH: The Voiced Ones".




## The Rite of Vocalscission

The Vocalscission, or the Rite of Silence, is the most sacred and foundational ritual in the world of HUSH. It is a procedure performed on every infant within the first few days of their life, and it is the very act that defines their society. The rite involves the delicate and precise severing of the vocal cords, rendering the individual incapable of speech for the rest of their lives. This is not seen as a mutilation, but as a holy act of purification, a way to sever the connection to the chaotic and primal world of sound and elevate the individual to a state of pure, unadulterated being.

The ceremony is performed by a special caste of healers known as the Silent Surgeons. These individuals are trained from a young age in the delicate art of the Vocalscission, and their hands are considered blessed. The procedure is performed in a sterile, temple-like environment, with the infant swaddled in white linens. The Silent Surgeon uses a small, obsidian blade, a tool that is believed to have been passed down from the first generation of Silent Surgeons. The cut is precise, and the healing process is carefully managed to ensure that the infant does not suffer any long-term health complications.

The philosophy behind the Vocalscission is deeply embedded in the world's religion and mythology. It is believed that the voice is a source of corruption, a tool that can be used to lie, to deceive, and to manipulate. By removing the voice, the society of HUSH believes that they have created a world free from these vices, a world where communication is based on pure, unfiltered emotion and intention, expressed through the body and the eyes. The Vocalscission is therefore not just a physical act, but a spiritual one, a way of ensuring that each individual is born into a world of truth and purity.




## The Silent Society

Life in the world of HUSH is a symphony of silence, a carefully choreographed dance of bodies and intentions. Without the spoken word, communication has evolved into a rich and nuanced tapestry of non-verbal cues. The primary mode of communication is a form of sign language, but it is far more than a simple set of gestures. It is a holistic language that incorporates the entire body, from the subtlest flicker of an eyelid to the most dramatic sweep of an arm. This language, known as "Asemic," is not taught in a formal setting but is absorbed from birth, a natural and intuitive part of their being.

The society is organized into small, tight-knit communities, where everyone knows everyone else. The lack of verbal deception has fostered a deep sense of trust and intimacy among the people. Relationships are built on a foundation of shared experiences and a profound understanding of each other's emotional states. The concept of privacy is different in this world; while physical privacy is respected, emotional privacy is almost non-existent. People can read each other's feelings with a clarity that would be unsettling to an outsider, but to them, it is the most natural thing in the world.

Art and music are also profoundly different in this silent world. Music is not auditory but is felt as vibrations, created by massive, resonating instruments that are played in communal spaces. These "Seismic Concerts" are a cornerstone of their culture, a way for the community to come together and experience a shared emotional journey. Visual art is highly prized, with a focus on hyper-realistic sculptures and paintings that capture the subtle nuances of human emotion. Storytelling is a visual and performative art, with troupes of actors and dancers who can convey epic tales without uttering a single sound.




## The Voiced Ones: Mythology and Role

The Voiced Ones are the stuff of legends, the boogeymen and goddesses of a world that has forgotten the sound of the human voice. They are the rare few women who, for reasons unknown, are born with the ability to vocalize. Each Voiced One is tied to a single, primal sound â€“ a moan, a scream, a laugh, a sigh. These sounds are not a choice but an involuntary expression of their deepest emotions. To the silent society, these women are an anomaly, a terrifying and fascinating throwback to a chaotic past.

The mythology surrounding the Voiced Ones is a complex tapestry of fear and reverence. On the one hand, they are seen as cursed, as vessels of a primal chaos that the world has long since abandoned. They are hunted and persecuted, their sounds seen as a corrupting influence that can shatter the fragile peace of the silent society. On the other hand, they are also worshipped in secret, seen as divine beings who hold the key to a lost world of passion and expression. Small, clandestine cults have formed around the Voiced Ones, who see their sounds as a form of divine communication, a direct link to the raw, untamed power of the universe.

The role of the Voiced Ones in this society is a paradoxical one. They are both outcasts and deities, feared and revered in equal measure. They live on the fringes of society, in hidden communities or as solitary wanderers. They are a constant reminder of what has been lost, a living embodiment of the power and the danger of the human voice. And for Hush, the one who can learn to mimic all their sounds, they are the key to unlocking her own destiny and changing the course of her world forever.




## A History of Silence

The world of HUSH was not always silent. There was a time, now lost to all but the most ancient and forbidden texts, when the world was filled with the cacophony of human voices. This was a time of great passion and great conflict, a time when words were used as both weapons and tools of creation. The histories of this era, known as the "Age of Chatter," are fragmented and contradictory, but they all speak of a world on the brink of collapse.

The turning point came with the arrival of a figure known as the First Silent Surgeon. He was a philosopher and a healer who believed that the human voice was the source of all the world's suffering. He preached a new philosophy of silence, a way of being that would transcend the limitations and corruptions of language. He and his followers developed the Rite of Vocalscission, and they began to perform it on their own children, creating the first generation of the silent society.

At first, they were a small and persecuted sect, but their message of peace and purity resonated with a world that was weary of conflict. The movement grew, and within a few generations, the Rite of Vocalscission had become the dominant practice. The Age of Chatter came to an end, and the Age of Silence began.

The first few centuries of the Age of Silence were a golden age. The world was at peace, and the silent society flourished. But as the centuries passed, the memory of the voice began to fade, and the Voiced Ones began to appear. At first, they were seen as a curiosity, a strange and beautiful anomaly. But as their numbers grew, so did the fear and suspicion. The Voiced Ones were cast out, and the mythology of their curse began to take shape.

Now, after a thousand years of silence, the world is on the brink of another transformation. The Voiced Ones are no longer just a myth; they are a reality. And in the person of Hush, they have found a champion, a voice that can unite them all and challenge the very foundations of the silent society.




## Geography and Regions

The world of HUSH, known to its inhabitants as "Alaya," is a single, massive continent surrounded by a vast, impassable ocean. The climate is temperate, with distinct seasons, but the geography is incredibly diverse, ranging from towering mountains to sprawling deserts.

**The Whispering Peaks:** A range of snow-capped mountains that bisect the continent. The peaks are perpetually shrouded in mist, and the winds that whip through the high passes are said to carry the echoes of the lost voices. The communities that live in the mountains are small and isolated, known for their stoicism and their deep connection to the natural world.

**The Silent Plains:** A vast, fertile grassland that stretches from the foothills of the Whispering Peaks to the shores of the Sunken Sea. This is the heartland of the silent society, where the largest and most prosperous communities are located. The plains are dotted with small, self-sufficient farming villages, and the landscape is dominated by the massive, resonating instruments that are used for their Seismic Concerts.

**The Sunken Sea:** A massive, inland sea that is believed to have been formed by a cataclysmic event in the distant past. The water is brackish and still, and the shores are lined with the ruins of ancient cities from the Age of Chatter. The Sunken Sea is a place of mystery and superstition, and it is said to be the home of strange, amphibious creatures.

**The Obsidian Desert:** A vast, black-sand desert that lies to the east of the Sunken Sea. The desert is a harsh and unforgiving environment, and it is home to nomadic tribes who have adapted to the extreme conditions. The Obsidian Desert is also the location of the hidden temples where the Silent Surgeons are trained.

**The Weeping Forest:** A dense, ancient forest that is located in the southern part of the continent. The trees in this forest are so tall and thick that they block out the sun, and the air is perpetually damp and cool. The Weeping Forest is a place of solitude and contemplation, and it is where many of the Voiced Ones have sought refuge.




## Social and Political Structures

The society of Alaya is both utopian and dystopian, a world without spoken conflict but with a rigid social order. The political structure is a decentralized theocracy, with each community governed by a council of elders. These elders are not elected but are chosen based on their wisdom, their empathy, and their mastery of Asemic, the silent language. Their role is not to rule but to guide, to mediate disputes, and to ensure that the community remains in harmony.

At the top of this theocracy are the Silent Surgeons, who are not just healers but also the spiritual leaders of the society. They are the guardians of the Rite of Vocalscission, and they hold the ultimate authority in all matters of faith and tradition. They live in secluded temples in the Obsidian Desert, and their pronouncements are carried by messengers to the far corners of the continent.

The social structure is communal and egalitarian, with a strong emphasis on cooperation and mutual support. There is no concept of private property; all resources are shared, and everyone contributes to the well-being of the community. The family unit is the core of the society, but the concept of family is broader than in our world. Children are raised by the entire community, and the bonds of kinship extend to everyone.

However, this utopian harmony comes at a price. The society is deeply conformist, and any form of individuality or dissent is seen as a threat to the collective. The Voiced Ones are the most extreme example of this, but anyone who deviates from the norm is at risk of being ostracized. The fear of chaos and the obsession with purity have created a society that is both peaceful and oppressive, a world where the absence of the voice has led to a different kind of silence: the silence of the individual spirit.




## Economy and Daily Life

The economy of Alaya is agrarian and localized. Each community is largely self-sufficient, producing its own food, clothing, and tools. The fertile land of the Silent Plains is the breadbasket of the continent, and the communities there trade their surplus crops with the more isolated communities in the mountains and the desert. The currency is not based on gold or silver but on a system of barter and exchange, where the value of goods is determined by their utility and their beauty.

Daily life is structured around the rhythms of nature and the needs of the community. The day begins at dawn, with a silent meditation that is practiced by everyone. The rest of the day is spent working in the fields, crafting goods, or maintaining the communal infrastructure. In the evenings, the community gathers for the Seismic Concerts, a time of shared emotional experience and social bonding.

The concept of work is different in this world. There is no distinction between labor and leisure; all activities are seen as a form of creative expression. The farmers who tend the fields see themselves as artists, sculpting the land to create a bountiful harvest. The weavers who create the intricate textiles are seen as storytellers, weaving the history and the myths of their people into the fabric.

This idyllic existence is not without its challenges. The reliance on localized agriculture makes the communities vulnerable to drought and famine. The lack of technological innovation means that life is often hard and laborious. And the ever-present threat of the Voiced Ones casts a long shadow over the peaceful society, a reminder of the chaos that lies just beneath the surface of their silent world.
