## Franchise Vision

HUSH: The Voiced Ones is positioned to become a global phenomenon with its unique premise, compelling characters, and expansive world. The franchise combines the dystopian appeal of "The Hunger Games" with the magical world-building of "Harry Potter," while offering a fresh perspective on voice, power, and resistance.

The tagline "In a world of silence, a whisper is a revolution" encapsulates the core theme that will resonate with audiences worldwide.
