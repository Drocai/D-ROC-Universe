# Echoverse - Series Context File

## What Is This
A 4-book science fiction series exploring consciousness, reality fractures, and the 3-6-9 pattern.

## Series Arc
1. **Echoes of None** - Introduction to the fractures, Aires & Dai emerge
2. **Echoes of Origin** - Discovering the source of the echoes
3. **Echoes of Convergence** - All timelines begin to merge
4. **Echoes of Transcendence** - Resolution and transcendence

## Key Characters
- **Aires** - Fracture entity
- **Dai** - Fracture entity (appears across ALL D-RoC stories)
- **Hum** - EXCLUSIVE to this series, do not use elsewhere

## Scientific Foundation
- 3-6-9 Pattern (Tesla's key to the universe)
- Consciousness as frequency
- Reality as malleable construct

## Rules
- "Hum" is BANNED outside Echoes of None
- Aires & Dai = fractures across ALL stories
- 3-6-9 Pattern = Core narrative device
